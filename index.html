üìä The Infrastructure Slice

Across all three foundations, package registry infrastructure represents only 6-15% of total budgets, despite serving billions of daily requests. The majority of spending goes to conferences, grants, and general operations. This creates a structural challenge: registry infrastructure competes for funding against more visible community activities. Compare to corporate-backed registries: npm (~$5M+, Microsoft) and Maven Central (~$3.5M+, Sonatype).
